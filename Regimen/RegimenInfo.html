Regimen helps you stay focused by keeping track of your daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, and long-term goals.

1. On the home screen, click on + at the top-right to add a goal.

2. Edit a goal by clicking on it.

3. Swipe right to complete a goal.

4. Swipe left to erase a goal.

5. Get stuff done and be productive.

Your list resets based on its duration, so daily goals refresh every day, weekly goals refresh every week, and so on.

Inspired by Marc Andreessen's personal guide to productivity.
